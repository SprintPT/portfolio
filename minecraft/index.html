About me

My name is Pedro Mota, also known as Sprint. I am a master degree student and part time 3D Modeler for Minecraft related projects. You can find some of my past works below. Feel free to reach out to me for questions related with my models or model requests!

Info: Apart from most of the models published on my Sketchfab or the kart pack, I will not redistribute or resell the models in my portfolio.
